Garbage is collected bi-weekly (every other week) on your regular collection day.

  • There is a four-bag limit per household. Each bag over the limit must be tagged with a Whitby-issued bag tag.
  • All garbage must be in bags. Garbage containers are not allowed in Whitby's collection program.
  • Maximum size of bags: 66 cm wide by 91 cm tall (26 inches by 36 inches) at the time of purchase. Oversized bags will not be collected.
  • Maximum weight per bag: 20 kilograms (44 pounds). Bags that break open when lifted will not be collected.
